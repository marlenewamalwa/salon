Our Story

Where every client
is treated like royalty

Marlene's Salon was born from a simple belief — that every woman deserves to feel her most beautiful self. Founded in 2012 by Marlene Wanjiku, we've grown from a small studio into Nairobi's most trusted beauty destination.

We invest in the finest products, ongoing training, and a warm, welcoming atmosphere that keeps our clients coming back again and again.

Certified & Trained Stylists All our team members are professionally certified with regular upskilling workshops.
Premium Products Only We use only top-tier, skin and hair-safe products from trusted international brands.
Hygiene is Our Priority Strict sanitation protocols, clean tools, and a spotless salon environment — always.
